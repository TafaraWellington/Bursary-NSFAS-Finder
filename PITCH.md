# Empower Your Grade 12s with the Bursary & NSFAS Finder

Every year, thousands of capable matriculants miss out on tertiary education simply because they don't know which bursaries they qualify for or how to apply for them. The **Bursary & NSFAS Finder** is a complete, white-labeled solution designed for high schools to bridge this gap.

## What is it?
The Bursary Finder is an intelligent, automated platform that matches your students' academic profiles and financial backgrounds to South Africa's top bursaries, including NSFAS, Sasol, Allan Gray, and more. 

## Key Features for Your School:
* **Bulk Import:** Upload your entire Grade 12 class list via CSV. The system instantly creates profiles and calculates matches for every student.
* **Smart Matching Algorithm:** Students are only shown the bursaries they actually qualify for, based on household income, matric subjects, and intended field of study.
* **AI-Powered Motivation Letters:** The platform uses advanced AI (Google Gemini) to generate personalized, first-draft motivation letters tailored to the specific bursary and student's profile.
* **Document Tracking:** A built-in checklist ensures students have certified ID copies, proof of income, and academic results ready before deadlines.
* **Automated Reminders:** WhatsApp and email alerts keep students on track as closing dates approach.

## Why Subscribe?

* **Save Teachers' Time:** Instead of teachers manually hunting for bursary forms, the platform automates the discovery and application prep process.
* **Custom Branded (White-Label):** The portal will feature your school's logo and colors, providing a premium experience for your students and parents.
